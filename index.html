Who We Are

We catalyze critical care breakthroughs

Forsch MedX is a healthcare innovation studio united by a shared mission: make intensive and acute care safer, smarter, and more human. We blend frontline clinical insight with rapid engineering sprints to deliver solutions that stay grounded in hospital realities.

Clinical-first discovery

We co-create with ICU teams, mapping workflows and root causes before sketching a single prototype. Every build starts with unmet patient and clinician needs.

Rapid, rigorous prototyping

Our hardware, software, and bioscience pods iterate in parallel, pressure-testing ideas through simulation, bench experiments, and pilot deployments.

Trusted partnerships

Backed by leading incubators and hospital collaborators, we align grants, mentors, and manufacturing partners to scale validated interventions responsibly.

Products

TrachEase

TrachEase is a smart solution designed to reduce ventilation associated pneumonia (VAP) in mechanically ventilated patients.

  • Customized Frequency

    Suction timing adapts to patient needs so care teams can tailor intervention frequency.

  • Reduced Infection

    Hands-free automation limits patient handling, lowering infection risk during airway care.

  • Hands-Free Procedure

    Automated clearing keeps the airway unobstructed so clinicians can focus on critical decisions.

CuraSpeXt

CuraSpeXt prepares the world for pandemics with rapid biosurveillance that spots emerging threats before they spread.

  • Real-time biosurveillance

    Continuous aerosol sensing tracks pathogens in critical hubs before clinical cases spike.

  • Edge-to-cloud analytics

    Secure pipelines push field signals into AI models that quantify risk in minutes.

  • Global readiness dashboards

    Actionable visualisations align public health, hospitals, and policy teams on response.
